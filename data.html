DATA PROCESS

1

DATA COLLECTION

Gathering relevant data from various sources, including APIs, databases, web scraping, and public datasets. Ensuring comprehensive coverage while respecting privacy and ethical considerations.

2

CLEANING & PREPARATION

Transforming raw data into a usable format through cleaning, normalization, and feature engineering. Handling missing values, outliers, and formatting inconsistencies to ensure analysis integrity.

3

EXPLORATORY ANALYSIS

Investigating data to discover patterns, anomalies, and relationships. Using statistical methods and visualizations to formulate hypotheses and identify promising analytical directions.

4

MODELING & ANALYSIS

Applying appropriate statistical or machine learning models to extract insights and make predictions. Iteratively refining approaches based on performance metrics and validation results.

5

VISUALIZATION & COMMUNICATION

Translating complex findings into clear, compelling visuals and narratives. Designing interactive visualizations that allow users to explore data from multiple perspectives and understand key insights.
